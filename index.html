TDWB is a top-down .io-style hero shooter. TDWB is currently in the Beta phase, with playable gameplay, but bugs and unfinished content. Designed for simplicity, TDWB is designed to be easy to learn and hard to master. Play TDWB with your keyboard and mouse, or a game controller.

Game Info

Instructions

Keyboard & Mouse

  • WASD - Move around
  • R - Reload Weapon
  • Move Mouse - Aim
  • Click - Shoot
  • Right Click - Aim
  • Z/X - Change Weapon
  • 1, 2, 3 - Fast Switch Weapon

Gamepad (XBOX)

  • Left Joystick - Move Around
  • X - Reload Weapon
  • Right Stick - Aim
  • Right Trigger - Shoot
  • Left Trigger - Aim
  • Left/Right Bumper - Change Weapon

Simplicity

TDWB has only 2 gameplay mechanics in battle: move and shoot. This makes TDWB easy to learn. When you've figured out your playstyle, find a hero that fits your style and play as them!

Online Multiplayer

TDWB is an online multiplayer game. You can invite your friends to play along with you, find others online, and even create private matches! The ranking system only takes into effect the number of kills you get before you die. If you don't get kills, your rank decreases. The more kills you get, the more your rank increases.

Unique Skins

We think you should be rewarded from your hard work, even if you lose. Gun skins are a way to show off your game playtime as well as skill. Find gun skins in the store and through TDWBoxes.

Lootbox System

We know it's frustrating to open a lootbox only to get an item you can't use, already own, or just don't like. Our lootbox system allows you to choose ANY eligible reward from lootboxes. Opening a legendary lootbox will always allow you to pick out the item you want!

Community

We know sometimes it can be hard finding people to play with, so join the Discord server to chat with the community, find major announcements, participate in events, and more. Make sure to invite your friends!

FAQ

Q: What is this game, anyways?
A: A replacement for shooters with pay-to-win, greed, bad game balancing, unfair lootboxes, and more.

Q: Who made this game?
A: This game is a collaboration between Themadpunter and lifesteal8. The game is published by Themadpunter Studios.

Q: How do you play?
A: The instructions can be found both in-game and on the itch.io page.

Q: What do you need to play this game?
A: A mildly strong computer (netbooks should work) with a web browser. Full system requirements available above.

Q: Do I need WiFi to play this game?
A: Yes. TDWB will not operate without some form of internet connection.

Q: How can I control this game?
A: You can use your keyboard and mouse, or a gamepad (e.g. XBOX controller, Joy-Cons). Mobile controls might exist someday.

Q: What can you pay for in this game?
A: There are no in-app purchases. The full version only gives you a starting advantage (you start with some credits without having to earn them).

Q: But I wanna pay to win!
A: You can't. Also, that's not a question.

Q: Who asked?
A: You. That's a question.

Stats & Info

Heroes

  • Kade is an Elite Necromancer. Kade heals 25% of all damage he deals, but has no passive healing.
  • Callsign S-36 is a SWAT sniper. Callsign can shoot through walls with their ghost bullets, but takes 50% more damage.
  • Zig is a former Olympic sprinter. Zig runs at double speed, but has a limited range with all his weapons.
  • Jordan is a retired military robot. Jordan moves at two thirds of normal speed, but takes less damage.

TDWBox Chances

TDWBoxes are an in-game way to earn rare skins. Each TDWBox starts off at Uncommon rarity, and has a 1/2 chance to increase the rarity, and a 1/2 chance to immediately open it without increasing the rarity. After opening a TDWBox, select one item from that rarity or below.

Rarity Chance
